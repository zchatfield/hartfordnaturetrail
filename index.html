South Hartford Nature Trail

Welcome!

The city of Hartford, Connecticut has a wealth of public green spaces. We invite you to explore the parks, community gardens, and natural hidden gems in the Southern part of Hartford.

Click on each green space to learn more about it, or use the search bar in the top left to look for your favorite spot. Click anywhere on the map to set the start and end for a route between any location.

This page is a collaborative project brought to you by Lauren Little Edutainment and Free Center. It was created by Zoe Chatfield and Emily Heltzel of Clark University.


Are there places we should add to the map? Do you have any favorite walking or biking routes you'd like to share?
